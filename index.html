FuelPass™: The R4–6B Fuel Wallet System

A live, working tool that calculates estimated fuel costs for trips across South Africa — designed for integration into banking and logistics platforms.

Users input their address, vehicle details, driving style, and frequency — and get an accurate, conservative estimate of fuel needed for daily, weekly, or monthly travel.

An exclusive national license is available, offering 10-year market exclusivity and first-mover advantage in a transformative revenue category.
